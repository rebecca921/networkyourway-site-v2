Who this is for

If networking
drains you.

Network Your Way is built for introverts, ambiverts, and reluctant networkers — people who are brilliant at what they do, but invisible in their market.

Not for you if

You love networking for its own sake, your week has unlimited evenings, or you want a glossy ebook with worksheets and a journal. (Honestly — go enjoy the room. We'll be over here.)

01

The reluctant networker

You're brilliant at the work. The room is the bit that drains you for two days afterwards. You want a way through that doesn't pretend the dread isn't there.

02

The introvert who's gone visible

You've moved out of in-house. Suddenly the LinkedIn posts and the breakfast events are on you. The wrong rooms feel especially expensive.

03

The ambivert running on fumes

You can do it. You've done it for years. You'd quite like a calendar that doesn't punish you on Wednesday for what you said yes to on Monday.

About Becs
