The Right Room
Framework.

Before you say yes to any event, ask yourself these four questions.
Two minutes now saves hours of wrong-room exhaustion.

👥
Question 1
People
Will there be people you actually want to meet or learn from?
Who's in the room — and do they matter to where you're going?
🎯
Question 2
Purpose
Does this event align with where you're trying to go right now?
Why this room, at this moment in your business?
⚡
Question 3
Energy
Will this room fill your tank or drain it? Format, crowd size, duration — it all counts.
How will you feel on the drive home?
💰
Question 4
Return
Is this worth the time, the cost, and the recovery? Most events are cheap — your energy isn't.
What does a good outcome actually look like?

Four questions. Two minutes. Right room every time.
