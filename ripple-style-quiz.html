Based on the principles behind Myers-Briggs and DISC, The Ripple Quiz reveals how you're genuinely wired for connection — not how good you are at networking, but how you naturally do it.

There are four styles. None is better than the others. All of them work. All of them have a blind spot. Knowing yours is where it gets interesting.

One honest note first: not knowing your style is quietly costing you visibility and opportunities, in rooms that were never built for how you're actually wired. This isn't about becoming someone else in a room. It's built for introverts, ambiverts, and recovering people-pleasers, the ones networking actually takes energy from.
