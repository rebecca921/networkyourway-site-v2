Quick questions

Before you get in touch.

Where is the coaching based?

I work with clients across the UK. Sessions are online unless otherwise agreed.

What's the difference between the cohort and 1:1 coaching?

The cohort follows the full six-stage Network Your Way arc in a small group — it's structured, progressive, and community-supported. 1:1 coaching is tailored entirely to you and your situation. Both are with me.

How do I know if I'm ready for 1:1 coaching?

If you have a clear sense that something needs to shift — in how visible you are, how you show up, or how you're building your network — you're probably ready. Just email me and we'll have an honest conversation about fit.

I'm not sure which option is right for me.

That's fine — just email hello@networkyourway.com and tell me where you are. I'll point you in the right direction, even if that's somewhere else entirely.
